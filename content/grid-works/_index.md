---
title: Grid Works
---

These designs were inspired by Ranko Bon, with whom I worked for many years. His passion for cave art and his imaginitive use of the simplest forms of expression eventually rubbed off on me. I started to produce these designs on my computer using Microsoft Word to have a bit of fun at Ranko's expense. But before long, I was drawn into this strange pursuit and became fascinated by the creation of different forms within a very simple framework. I settled for pixel-editing these in MS Paint. Very simple images at 84 x 60 pixels.
