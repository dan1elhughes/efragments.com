---
title: Grid Works
---

These designs were inspired by a colleague of mine, Ranko Bon, with whom I worked for many years. His imaginitive use of the simplest forms of expression  rubbed off on me. I started to produce these designs using Microsoft Word to have a bit of fun at hiss expense. Before long, I was drawn into this strange pursuit and became fascinated by the creation of different forms within a very simple framework. I moved to pixel editing in MS Paint, then to a pixel editor app in Android. Very simple images at 84 x 60 pixels, the 6x4 grid can be divided into many forms. Black and white dominate, often with confusion about whether it is black on white or white on black. The only colour used for accents or highlights is red, which should never touch black. Every element is a multiple of 3 pixels, except where the numbers don't add up.
